Explanation of re.findall(r'\b[Pp]art\s\d+\b', text)
üìå Breakdown of the Regular Expression Pattern:

re.findall(r'\b[Pp]art\s\d+\b', text)

Component	Explanation
re.findall(...)	Extracts all matches of the pattern from text.
r''	Raw string notation, ensuring that \ characters are treated as literal.
\b	Word boundary, ensures it matches a whole word and not part of another word.
[Pp]	Matches uppercase or lowercase 'P', allowing both "Part 91" and "part 121".
art	Matches the exact word "art", ensuring the full word "Part" is captured.
\s	Matches a space character (" "), ensuring "Part" is followed by a space.
\d+	Matches one or more digits (0-9), capturing numbers like "91" or "121".
\b	Word boundary again, ensuring "Part 121" is matched as a whole word, not part of "Part121XYZ".

Expected Matches:

Text Sample	Captured Match
"Refer to Part 91 regulations."	"Part 91"
"The requirements are in part 121."	"part 121"
"Follow part 135 or part 145."	"part 135", "part 145"
"This is not Part121."	‚ùå (No match, missing space)
